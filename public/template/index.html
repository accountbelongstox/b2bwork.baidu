Cryptocurrency Prices FAQs

  • How do you calculate crypto market cap?
    A cryptocurrencys market capitalization, or market cap, is calculated by multiplying its circulating supply by its current price at any given moment.
  • What are the top 10 cryptocurrencies?

    Generally, top cryptocurrencies are ranked by market cap. This page can show you the top 10 cryptocurrencies by market cap, and you can sort the list in ascending or descending order.

    Alternatively, you can also view the top cryptocurrencies by price performance using the "Todays change" column. Sorting in ascending and descending order is also available for this column.

  • What determines cryptocurrency price changes?

    Cryptocurrency prices are reflective of the current supply and demand trends for that digital asset. If buying pressure in the market outweighs selling pressure, the price will rise. Similarly, when sellers outnumber buyers, the price falls.

    Unlike fiat currencies, many cryptocurrencies, such as BTC,DOT,SOL, often have a fixed total supply or issuance rate. Since price is related to supply and demand, a fixed or limited supply can also affect the price of an asset by making it more rare.

    If you want to invest in a cryptocurrency from the list on this page, but dont see it in the "Buy/Sell" section, that means youll need to use a top cryptocurrency like BTC or USDT to buy the asset. Select "Trade" near any asset on this page to buy it for crypto.

  • Can I earn a yield on my cryptocurrency investment?

    Many cryptocurrencies generate a yield for holders who participate in various network activities, such as staking. However, staking, as well as providing liquidity on DeFi protocols, can be confusing and expensive.

    At OKX, we bring you easy, flexible, less expensive access to a variety of yield-generating opportunities for many top cryptocurrencies.
